**Bull case:** Padel is the fastest-growing sport in Europe — 25% YoY participation growth. Playtomic has 80%+ market share in club booking across Southern Europe. Network effects: more players attract more clubs, more clubs attract more players. Recurring revenue from subscriptions and transaction fees.

**Bear case:** Low regulatory moat — booking is not a regulated activity. Vulnerable to Google or a larger marketplace entering. International expansion requires local club acquisition which is capital-intensive.

**Competitive landscape:** Matchi (Nordics-focused), PadelMania (Spain-only). No dominant global player. Club operators use fragmented legacy systems or pen-and-paper. Playtomic is the clear European leader.

**Product maturity:** Live. 8M+ registered users. 12,000+ clubs. Active in 20+ countries. Mobile app rated 4.7+ on both stores.

**TAM estimate:** $20B+ global racket sports and fitness booking market. Padel alone growing 25% YoY in Europe.

**Revenue model:** SaaS subscription for clubs (booking management) + transaction fees on player bookings. Dual revenue stream.

**Distribution:** Product-led growth — players discover Playtomic to book courts, then pull their clubs onto the platform. Network effects compound.

**Distribution advantage:** 80%+ market share in Southern European club booking. Network effects: more players attract more clubs.

**Investor quality:** GP Bullhound (led Series A), BStartup (Sabadell). Credible European investors with marketplace experience.

**Exit comparables:** ClassPass (acquired by Mindbody for $500M+), Zwift (IPO-track at $1B+ valuation), Sportradar (NYSE-listed, $3B+ market cap)
